Pricing Info

Booking

Half of your total is due upon booking as a deposit to reserve your date.

The other half is due two months prior to your event's date.

If there is a cancellation less than three months prior, the full booking deposit will b non-refundable.

If a cancellation is made prior to three months, half of the deposit will be refunded.

Email transfer freddysonmain@gmail.com include name/dates in notes.

If Freddy's isn't booked the day before your event, we are happy to include it free in your booking.

If another party inquires about booking that date, we will first offer it to you at the regular rate before allowing another party to book it.

Groups have worked together successfully in the past to and shared tables, chairs and decor to allow their weddings to take place back to back very conveniently.
